Current Threat Landscape

HIGH SEVERITY

Ransomware Evolution

Advanced ransomware groups are using double and triple extortion tactics, targeting backup systems and threatening data leaks.

HIGH SEVERITY

Supply Chain Attacks

Attackers are increasingly targeting software supply chains to compromise multiple organizations through trusted vendors.

MEDIUM SEVERITY

Cloud Misconfigurations

Exposed cloud storage buckets and misconfigured services continue to be a major source of data breaches.

MEDIUM SEVERITY

Advanced Phishing Campaigns

Modern phishing attacks leverage artificial intelligence, deepfake technology, and sophisticated social engineering to bypass traditional security awareness training. Business Email Compromise (BEC) attacks use AI-generated content mimicking executives' writing styles. Vishing (voice phishing) employs deepfake audio to impersonate trusted contacts. SMS phishing (smishing) uses urgent messages with malicious links. These attacks often bypass email security gateways using legitimate services like Google Forms or Microsoft 365. Defense requires multi-layered approaches including advanced email security, user behavior analytics, phishing simulation training, zero-trust email policies, and incident response procedures for credential compromise scenarios.
